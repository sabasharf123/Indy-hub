Services

IndyHub provides independent workers with the support they need allowing them to focus on the job, not the admin. We provide incorporation, banking, accounting, web design and promotion services allowing you to manage and organize them in one place. We also promise award winning customer service as we know how precious your time is.

Advocacy

We work to create better working conditions for freelancers. This involves lobbying state and federal government for better policies as well as working with you to create the industries norms you want to see.

Advice

We offer free, impartial advice written by freelancers who face the same issues you have and know what it's like. We also give access to experts who can advise you on legal, accounting, and client issues.

Our Story

Freelancing as consultants for several years now, Co-Founders Alissa Orlando and Owen Ensor have become passionate about their work they create for others. However, they quickly became aware of issues that hindered them from success, such as clients not paying them, working without contracts, and having to adapt to working independently without an office space. It was of no surprise that other independent workers were facing similar issues.

With no representation or platform for support, Alissa and Owen decided to create their own.

IndyHub is a startup of four empowered entrepreneurs and engineers. Along with Owen and Alissa, Saba Sharfuddin and Pallav Agarwal are engineers looking to disrupt the freelancing industry through code.

Our mission is simple. We want to minimize the cost and time to be your own boss and promote fair, equitable, and dignified practices among our members. We want to achieve so by creating a single point to find advice, manage administrative tasks and engage with a community. On this website, you will not only find all the answers to your questions, but you will find an inspiring community for support and for representation.
